About me:

If, somehow, you've come across this website, then I suppose I'm glad to welcome you.

My initial thoughts going into this is to create some place where my thoughts can exist outside of my mind. To give my thoughts permanence, beyond what's scribbled in a .txt file without refinement and promptly left to decay in a desktop folder.

I've developed a great repository of personal memorandums, creating a profile of nearly every meaningful or absurd thought I've experienced over the last few years -- some of which I'd love to revise into something which isn't abrasive scrawl stashed in cloud storage under my bed sheets.

I'm a lazy bastard, so much so that often chest-constricting quantities of caffeine is seldom enough to dissolve my fatigue. The construction of this site will certainly take some time, but if you care to be patient, or check in every now and again, perhaps you will be blessed with the opportunity to gawk at the mind of a lunatic, who only manages to brave the day because he is lucid enough to see that.
